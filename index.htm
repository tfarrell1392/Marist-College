Marist College is a secondary school for boys in Athlone,County Westmeath,Ireland

the school was founded in 1884 by the Marist Brothers, a French Order, and was the frist principal was Brother Mungo. The original school was based in Glesson Street/Saint Marys Square. The college had a seminary for Juniorate training between 1915 and 1936 located at Bailieborough castle, County Caven

In 1973, the school moved to its current location on Retreet Road. The current principal is Micheal Dermondy and the deputy principal is Paul Kelly
